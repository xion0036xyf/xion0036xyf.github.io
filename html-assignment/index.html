About Me

As a newcomer,I have arrived in Canada for about three months.Before studying in AC, I worked as an academic editor in China. This is the 8th year since I graduated from school. It has been a long time since I studied and worked in Chinese university. And now I returned to campus life, just in different country. For me, this experience feels both familiar and strange.

When I first arrived in Ottawa, the unfamiliar environment and cuisine frustrated me, as it was my first time traveling abroad alone. I felt lonely and anxious. One night, in the middle of the night, I missed my family deeply. Then, I noticed some comments on my social media account.

Do not hope too much, and also do not disappoint too much. Keep learning, learning, and observing. Allow yourself to make mistakes. -- a stranger
I had never met this person, nor had I ever spoken to him before. However, his kind words encouraged me to adjust to my new life in Canada. I truly cherish these words.
