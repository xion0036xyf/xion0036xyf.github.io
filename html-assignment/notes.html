5 Html Tags

<p>
Paragraphs, for adding a paragraph of text we can use the <p>tag.
<q>
Quote, a small quotation embedded within other content.
<blockquote>
a large, stand alone quote from another source.
<em>
Emphasis, a string of emphasized, slightly more important text.
<strong>
Important text, a string of highly emphasized, much more important text.
